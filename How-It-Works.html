How Skinks Works

A simple, secure journey for both hirers and local talent.

Step 1 (For Hirers)

Post Your Task

Describe what you need, set your budget (fixed or hourly), and define the location. It's free to post!

Step 2 (For Talent)

Get Notified & Apply

Create your profile, get verified, and receive real-time alerts for local tasks. Apply to jobs you want.

Step 3 (For Hirers)

Hire the Best Fit

Chat privately, compare offers from local talent, and hire the person best for your job.

Step 4 (Everyone)

Complete & Pay Securely

Once the task is done, the Hirer releases the payment, and the Talent gets paid. Simple and secure.
