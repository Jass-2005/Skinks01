Our Mission

"Every community has hidden talent. Skinks helps you discover it."

The Hyperlocal Problem

We saw a major disconnect. Global freelancing platforms are amazing, but they're too broad for everyday, local needs. Finding a reliable electrician, a last-minute photographer, or a math tutor in your own neighborhood was surprisingly difficult.

Tasks went unfulfilled while skilled neighbors (students, homemakers, local freelancers) missed out on micro-income opportunities right next door.

Our Solution

Skinks was born to bridge this gap. We're a hyperlocal-first platform focused on connecting you with verified talent within a 2-5km radius. Our goal is to build community trust, empower local economies, and make collaboration as simple as walking down the street.
